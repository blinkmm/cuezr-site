The Problem

Tracking multiple Powerpoint® machines can be a challenge
and tracking video playback in powerpoint is even harder

In live production, not knowing is the risk.. How much time is left? What slide are we on? Is backup in sync? CUEZR makes the invisible visible giving your team real-time clarity so every cue lands exactly when it should.

What You Get

Built for operators who
can't afford surprises.

Slide Visibility

Current slide number, total slides, and deck name — live. Always know exactly where you are in your presentaion.

Video Countdown

Total duration and time remaining for every video clip, so your whole crew is ready for the next cue the moment it ends.

Main + Backup Monitoring

Both systems side by side in one view. Spot drift between machines instantly and intervene before the audience ever notices.

At-a-Glance Show Status

Dark UI, big numbers, zero clutter. Designed to be read from across a production desk or fed into a switcher multiview.
